Position Title: Marketing Manager

Brief description of the position: The Marketing Manager will be responsible for developing and implementing marketing strategies to promote Ceylon Textile Suppliers' products and services.

Salary Range: $80,000 - $100,000 per annum

Reporting to: General Manager

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and implement marketing strategies and campaigns
  • Conduct market research and analyze trends to identify new opportunities
  • Manage marketing budget and allocate resources effectively
  • Create and oversee marketing materials and content
  • Collaborate with internal teams and external partners
  • Monitor and report on the effectiveness of marketing campaigns

essential qualifications, skills, knowledge, and attributes for a marketing manager may include:

qualifications :

  • possess a bachelor's degree in marketing, business administration, or a similar subject. Master's degree is an advantage.

Skills :

  • excellent interpersonal, communication, and leadership abilities..
  • excellent analytical and problem-solving abilities..
  • competent in both project and budget management..
  • knowledge of market research, data analysis, and consumer insights..
  • Possibility of original, creative thought..
  • Excellent time-management and organizational abilities.
  • excellent computer abilities, including mastery of Microsoft Office and marketing platforms like Hubspot, Marketo, or Salesforce.

Knowledge:

  • thorough understanding of the finest practices, principles, and methods in marketing.
  • solid familiarity with the most recent marketing trends, technology, and channels.
  • knowing how to use digital marketing tools like email, social media, mobile marketing, and search engine marketing.
  • knowledge of marketing metrics and analytics to assess the success and return on investment of campaigns.

Attributes:

  • imaginative and creative thinking.
  • Self-starter having the capacity to function alone.
  • capable of setting priorities and meeting deadlines; results-oriented.
  • strong managerial and team-building abilities.
  • the capacity to change with the market and customer trends.
  • solid business sense and strategic planning.

qualifications :

Position Title: Quality control manager

Brief description of the position: The Quality Control Manager for Ceylon Textile Suppliers (CTS) is responsible for ensuring all products meet customer expectations, company standards, and regulatory requirements. They will work closely with production teams to provide feedback and training, and manage a team of quality control technicians or inspectors. The successful candidate must have excellent communication skills, attention to detail, and the ability to analyze data and implement process improvements.

Salary Range: $100,000 - $120,000 per annum

Reporting to: General Manager

Key Responsibilities:

  • establishing and putting into practice quality control standards and processes to guarantee that all goods satisfy or surpass client needs, business standards, and legal obligations.
  • inspecting goods and production methods to look for issues or faults.
  • examining inspection data to provide reports on quality control performance, trends, and areas for improvement.
  • working directly with the production teams to give feedback, training, and guidance on quality control standards and practices, as well as to continually enhance the manufacturing process to eliminate errors and decrease waste.
  • managing and developing a group of quality control specialists or inspectors to make sure they are prepared and driven to carry out their tasks with the utmost skill and effectiveness.
  • coordinating with suppliers and clients to address quality control concerns and put remedial measures into place.
  • ensuring adherence to all necessary quality control guidelines and standards, including ISO and other sector-specific certifications..
  • the company's quality management system is kept up to date and improved, and frequent internal audits are conducted to assure continued compliance and development.

Essential qualifications, skills, knowledge, and attributes for a quality control manager typically include:

qualifications :

  • a bachelor's degree in a Textile Engineering, Materials Science relevant subject, like engineering or quality control.
  • An accreditation in quality management, such as the ASQ Certified Manager of Quality/Organizational Excellence

Skills :

  • outstanding interpersonal and leadership abilities
  • powerful analytical and problem-solving abilities
  • the capacity to prioritize and handle several initiatives at once proficiency in statistical analysis and textile-specific quality control techniques
  • Excellent communication abilities, both in writing and speaking.

Knowledge:

  • comprehensive understanding of textile production processes and quality assurance techniques
  • a working knowledge of textile testing procedures and standards like AATCC or ASTM
  • knowledge of the performance qualities and fabric attributes
  • understanding of the rules and regulations governing textiles, including REACH and Oeko-Tex

Attributes:

  • precision and close attention to detail
  • a capacity for both autonomous and teamwork
  • high standards of quality and a strong work ethic
  • Flexibility and flexibility in a hectic setting
  • a focus on the needs of the consumer and a dedication to fulfilling or surpassing those needs

Preferable qualifications, skills, knowledge, and attributes for a quality control manager may include:

qualifications :

  • a master's degree in materials science, textile engineering, or a closely related topic
  • AATCC or ASTM certification for textile testing

Skills :

  • Knowledge of project management
  • Training and improvement of quality control staff's skills
  • knowledge of data management and analysis software for the textile industry

Knowledge:

  • being familiar with international textile quality standards like ISO 17025 or OHSAS 18001
  • knowledge of certification programs for sustainable textile manufacture, such as GOTS or Bluesign
  • knowledge of technological advances and textile machinery

Attributes:

  • Problem-solving and process innovation via creativity
  • Ability to discover chances for process and product development via an entrepreneurial mentality and business acumen
  • Ability to collaborate across departments and with suppliers via cross-functional cooperation and communication

Experience : Depending on the business and the particular function, the number of years of experience needed for a quality control manager in textile suppliers may change. A quality control manager at a textile supplier, according to our company normally requires at least 5-7 years of experience in textile quality control or a related subject.
